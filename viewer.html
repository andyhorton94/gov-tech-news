How this service works

This is a tool that aggregates news and reports from various UK government and policy websites into one easy-to-read interface.

Where does the news come from?

  • GOV.UK Blog
  • National Audit Office
  • Institute for Government
  • techUK
  • Policy Exchange

How it works

A scraper collects articles published within the last 7 days and categorizes them automatically based on keywords into "Tech", "Farming", and "Economic" tags.

Features

  • Filtering: Use the dropdown and buttons above to filter by source and category.
  • Split View: Click the split icon to read articles side-by-side without opening new tabs.
  • Export: Save your current filtered view as a standalone HTML file.
